data synesthesia
[work]

a VR environment created in Blender, this project mediates ryoji ikeda’s Datamatics, which explores the relationship between data, sound, and image. using raw data like genomes or topographic maps, datamatics transforms information into geometric visuals and sound, inviting reflection on how we perceive and interact with data. i chose to place this mediation in a familiar, everyday environment, the subway, where elements gradually transform in sync with the music. the VR format immerses the viewer, allowing them to experience this audiovisual transformation directly and intensely.



software: Blender | After Effects
