unrepeated is a mini edition of eight objects, ranging from leather heels to glass plates, a dagger, and even skin, each engraved with the same motif.
I explore how different materials distort the pattern, giving rise to new forms. the edition brings together eight unique works, each revealing a version of the motif that drifts further or closer to the original, highlighting the dialogue between motif, material, and transformation.
