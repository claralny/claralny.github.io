HOW IT SOUNDED WHEN IT DRIED
[work]

this project reimagines the spray, a quick, unpredictable street art gesture, in digital and augmented reality. particles move and transform, creating an interactive space where sound and motion collide.
I also created a poster and fanzine: the poster features scannable images that trigger the AR experience, while the fanzine transforms the eight sounds into eight sentences that together form a short story.



softwares: InDesign | Touch Designer | Artivive | Photoshop
