HOW IT SOUNDED WHEN IT DRIED
[work]

this project reimagines the spray, a quick, unpredictable gesture from street art, in digital and augmented reality. particles move, shift, and transform, extending the gesture into a living, evolving space where sound and motion collide. result is an interactive, immersive experience where rhythm, randomness, and control merge, giving the spray a new life beyond the physical canvas.



software: InDesign | Touch Designer | Artivive | Photoshop
